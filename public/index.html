Facilitated outcome-based land management

About this prototype

This map displays information on the potential of changes in land management to deliver a wider range of environmental and financial outcomes. Read more about this research project which is funded by the Scottish Government.

To use this application, zoom to an area by selecting a featured location. Then, draw a custom shape to get a list of information related to your area of interest.

Partnerships

We are working with colleagues from Scotland's two national parks to develop and test this tool. More information on the Cairngorms National Park and the Loch Lomond & Trossachs National Park.

Ongoing research

This application is under active development, so it will change and there maybe parts e.g. buttons or links that do not work.
